Method

How I approach projects

I focus on practical business use: what information needs to be visible, who owns the next step, and how a workflow or report can help people act.

01

Define the business question or process problem.

02

Organize the source data, fields, owners, and status categories.

03

Build a usable dashboard, tracker, workflow, or documentation structure.

04

Translate the output into clear next actions for the team.

05

Document how the system works and what should be improved next.
